In December, we hosted the Christmas Horror Competition, an event that was as exciting as it was rewarding—not just for the team at Decadent Serpent, but also for our wonderful audience. Now, the time has come for you to decide the winner!

### Voting Rules:

- **The audience decides the winner!**

- To cast your vote, you must follow us on social media—either on X or Instagram.

- Each person can vote for only one entry.

- Simply fill out the form below to submit your vote.

### Important Details:

- **Voting Deadline**: Make sure to cast your vote by 02/01/2025 to ensure your favourite entry has a chance to win!

- **Spread the Word**: Share the link to the voting form with your friends and invite them to join the fun. Every vote counts!

- **The Prize**: The winning entry will receive a £50 reward so choose wisely!

To view the list of entries and cast your vote, simply scroll down. A huge thank-you to all our incredible participants for their spine-chilling, creative entries.
