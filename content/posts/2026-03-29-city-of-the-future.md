_Author: Weston Newbell_

A car glides at speeds unknown,
Its purple lights, flickering beneath it,
Blasting music of a new era, of tech alone.
A car glides at speeds unknown,
The driver sitting on a leather throne.
The city buries, it devours the steel chariot,
A car glides at speeds unknown,
Its purple lights, flickering beneath it.
