_Author: Weston Newbell_

```
A car glides at speeds unknown,Its purple lights, flickering beneath it,Blasting music of a new era, of tech alone.A car glides at speeds unknown,The driver sitting on a leather throne.The city buries, it devours the steel chariot,A car glides at speeds unknown,Its purple lights, flickering beneath it.
```

_Weston Newbell has been reading and writing poetry for several years on his Substack under the handle @westonzebbra. Last year, he published a collection of his poems, titled Violet Sorrows: Collected Poems, which is available for purchase on Amazon. He continues to write poems on themes of love, sorrow, spirituality, technology, and whatever else finds his interest._
