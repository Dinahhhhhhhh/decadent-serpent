**Privacy Policy for Decadent Serpent (DS)**

Effective Date: 04/10/2024

Decadent Serpent (DS) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services.

**1\. Information We Collect**

**1.1. Personal Information**

- When you subscribe to our newsletter, submit content, or contact us, we may collect personal information, including:
    - Name
    
    - Email address
    
    - Any additional details you provide in communication.

**1.2. Non-Personal Information**

- We automatically collect certain data when you visit our site, such as:
    - IP address
    
    - Browser type
    
    - Device information
    
    - Pages visited and time spent on the site.

**2\. How We Use Your Information**

We use your data to:

- Communicate with you about your submissions, inquiries, or subscriptions.

- Make hiring decisions, decide on retention and other human resources related decisions

- Improve our website and content through analytics.

- Notify you of updates, events, or new features (if you’ve opted in).

- Ensure compliance with legal obligations and terms of use.

**3\. Sharing Your Information**

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with:

- Service providers who help us manage our website or send communications.

- Legal authorities if required to comply with laws or regulations.

**4\. Your Data Rights**

You have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal information.

- Withdraw consent for communications at any time.

- Contact us at [editor@decadentserpent.com](mailto:editor@decadentserpent.com) to exercise these rights.

**5\. Cookies and Tracking**

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and collect analytics data. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

**6\. Security**

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

**7\. Updates to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

**8\. Contact Us**

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at editor@decadentserpent.com.
